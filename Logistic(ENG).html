LOGISTICS TERMINOLOGY

EXW (Ex Works)

SELLER:

Sell goods at the seller's warehouse

(FOB, CIF, CFR, DAP, DDP)
BUYER:
  1. Transport goods from the warehouse to the port/airport
  2. Customs clearance procedures
  3. Purchase shipping costs
  4. Customs clearance procedures
  5. Transport goods from the port/airport to the buyer's warehouse
  6. Insurance: purchase or not purchase

FOB (Free On Board)

SELLER:

Deliver goods at the seller's port (on board ship/airplane)

  1. Transport goods from the warehouse/airport to the port
  2. Customs clearance procedures
BUYER:
  1. Purchase shipping costs from port/airport A-B
  2. Customs clearance procedures
  3. Transport goods from the port/airport to the warehouse
  4. Insurance: purchase or not purchase

CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight)

SELLER:

Deliver goods of the buyer and include insurance

  1. Transport goods from the warehouse to the port/airport
  2. Customs clearance procedures
  3. Purchase shipping costs
  4. Insurance
BUYER:
  1. Customs clearance procedures
  2. Transport goods from the port/airport to the warehouse

CFR (Cost and Freight)

SELLER:

Deliver goods and pay shipping costs to the destination port

  1. Transport goods from the warehouse to the port of export
  2. Customs clearance procedures
  3. Shipping costs at the port of export
BUYER:
  1. Insurance costs
  2. Unloading costs at the destination port
  3. Customs clearance procedures
  4. Transport goods from the port to the warehouse

DAP (Delivered At Place)

SELLER:

Deliver goods at the buyer's specified location

  1. Transport goods from the warehouse to the port of export
  2. Customs clearance procedures
  3. International shipping costs
  4. Transport to the specified location
BUYER:
  1. Customs clearance procedures
  2. Import taxes and fees
  3. Unloading costs at the destination

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

SELLER:

Deliver goods and pay taxes at the buyer's location

  1. All transportation costs
  2. Customs clearance procedures
  3. Import taxes and fees
  4. Deliver goods to the buyer's location
BUYER:

Receive goods at the agreed location
