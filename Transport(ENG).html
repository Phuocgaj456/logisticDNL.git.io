Explosive & Drug

Prohibited explosives:

  • Fireworks and various types of explosives
  • Military and civilian explosives
  • Industrial and civilian explosives
  • Explosive devices and slow-burning wires
  • Homemade explosive materials

High risk of explosion during transportation

Serious consequences:

  • Causes injury and damage to people and property
  • Violation of criminal law
  • Punishment of fines and imprisonment
×

Compressed Gas & Pressure Tank

Prohibited compressed gases:

  • All types of gas cylinders
  • High pressure oxygen, nitrogen, CO2 cylinders
  • Flammable compressed gas cylinders
  • Aerosol cans
  • Toxic and corrosive gases

Risk factors:

  • High pressure can cause explosion
  • Flammable when in contact with heat
  • Leakage can cause suffocation

Can explode under high pressure and temperature

×

Flammable Liquids

Prohibited flammable liquids:

  • Gasoline and petroleum products
  • Industrial and medical alcohol
  • Organic solvents
  • Paint and varnish
  • Fuel

Safety risks:

  • Flammable at room temperature
  • Toxic fumes can cause poisoning
  • Reacts with other substances

Extremely flammable and can cause explosions

×

Toxic Substances

Prohibited toxic substances:

  • Pesticides and herbicides
  • Industrial hazardous chemicals
  • Biological toxins
  • Strong acids and bases
  • Carcinogenic substances

Health risks:

  • Acute poisoning
  • Organ damage
  • Nervous system effects

Severe poisoning when in contact

Radioactive Substances

Prohibited radioactive materials:

  • Radioactive materials
  • Medical radioactive devices
  • Radioactive waste
  • Nuclear fuel
  • Radioisotopes

Radiation effects:

  • Genetic mutation
  • Cell damage
  • Cancer and blood diseases

Radiation causes serious and long-term damage
