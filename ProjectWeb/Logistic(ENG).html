LOGISTICS TERMINOLOGY

EXW (Ex Works)

NGƯỜI BÁN:

Selling goods at the seller's warehouse

(Factory Price, Warehouse Price)
BUYER:
  1. Transport from warehouse to port/airport
  2. Customs clearance
  3. Purchase shipping
  4. Customs clearance
  5. Transport from port/airport to buyer's warehouse
  6. Insurance: buy or not buy

FOB (Free On Board)

NGƯỜI BÁN:

Deliver goods at the seller's port (on board ship/airplane)

  1. Transport from warehouse/airport to port
  2. Customs clearance
NGƯỜI MUA:
  1. Purchase shipping from port/airport A-B
  2. Customs clearance
  3. Transport from port/airport to buyer's warehouse
  4. Insurance: buy or not buy

CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight)

NGƯỜI BÁN:

Deliver goods to buyer and include insurance

  1. Transport from warehouse to port/airport
  2. Customs clearance
  3. Purchase shipping
  4. Insurance
BUYER:
  1. Customs clearance
  2. Transport from port/airport to buyer's warehouse

CFR (Cost and Freight)

NGƯỜI BÁN:

Deliver goods and pay freight to destination port

  1. Transport from warehouse to export port
  2. Customs clearance
  3. Freight charges at export port
  4. International shipping
BUYER:
  1. Insurance charges
  2. Freight charges at destination port
  3. Customs clearance
  4. Transport from destination port to buyer's warehouse

DAP (Delivered At Place)

NGƯỜI BÁN:

Deliver goods to buyer's specified location

  1. Transport from warehouse to export port
  2. Customs clearance
  3. International shipping
  4. Transport to buyer's specified location
BUYER:
  1. Customs clearance
  2. Import tax and fee
  3. Freight charges at destination point

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)

BUYER:

Deliver goods and pay taxes at buyer's location

  1. All transportation costs
  2. Customs clearance
  3. Import tax and fee
  4. Deliver goods to buyer's location
BUYER:

Receive goods at agreed location
