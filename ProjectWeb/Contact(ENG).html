General FAQ

How to track my order?

+

To track your order, you can:

  • Enter the tracking number on the website
  • Use the LogiTrack mobile app
  • Contact the hotline 1800 1234

Average shipping time?

+

Shipping time depends on the route:

  • Within the city: 1-2 days
  • Between provinces: 2-3 days
  • International: 5-7 working days

Compensation policy for goods?

+

We have a clear compensation policy:

  • Compensate 100% of the declared value if the goods are lost
  • Compensate according to the actual damage level
  • Resolve complaints within 24h

When do I need to pay shipping fees?

+

There are 2 payment methods:

  • Prepayment: Customer pays when placing an order
  • Payment after: Payment when receiving goods (COD)

How is shipping fee calculated?

+

Shipping fee is calculated based on:

  • Weight of the cargo
  • Distance of the shipment
  • Shipping method
  • Additional services (if any)

Refund policy?

+

We commit to refund in the following cases:

  • Not delivered: Refund 100%
  • Late delivery: Refund according to compensation policy
  • Damaged goods: Refund according to damage level

Refund time: 3-5 working days
